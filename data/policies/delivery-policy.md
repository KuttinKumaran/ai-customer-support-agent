# Samsung D2C Delivery Policy

## Standard Delivery Timelines

- Metro cities: 3-5 business days
- Tier 2/3 cities: 5-7 business days
- Large appliances (TVs, refrigerators): 7-10 business days

## Delayed Delivery Compensation

If your order delivery is delayed beyond the promised date:

- **3 or more days late**: You are eligible for a **₹500 coupon** applicable on your next Samsung D2C purchase.
- **7 or more days late**: You are eligible for a **full refund** or replacement at your choice.

## Tracking

All orders include real-time tracking via our courier partners. You will receive SMS and email updates at each milestone.

## Contact

For delivery issues, contact Samsung D2C support with your Order ID. Our AI support agent can assist 24/7.
