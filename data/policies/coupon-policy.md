# Samsung D2C Coupon Compensation Policy

## Delay Compensation Coupons

When delivery is delayed by 3 to 6 days beyond the promised date, customers receive a **₹500 coupon**.

## Coupon Terms

- Valid for 90 days from issue date
- Applicable on Samsung D2C purchases of ₹5,000 or more
- Cannot be combined with other offers
- One coupon per delayed order

## How to Redeem

The coupon code is sent via email and SMS. Enter the code at checkout on samsung.com/in.

## Escalation

If delay exceeds 7 days, the coupon is superseded by full refund eligibility per the Refund Policy.
