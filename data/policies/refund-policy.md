# Samsung D2C Refund Policy

## Eligibility

Full refunds are available when:

- The order is delayed by **7 or more days** beyond the promised delivery date
- The product arrives damaged or defective
- The wrong product was delivered
- The order was cancelled before shipment

## Refund Process

1. Refund request is verified against order and shipment records
2. Approved refunds are processed within **5-7 business days**
3. Amount is credited to the original payment method

## Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may apply for opened products returned within the 10-day return window, subject to inspection.

## Non-Refundable

- Customized or personalized products
- Products damaged due to customer misuse
